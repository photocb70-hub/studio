
# Changelog

All notable changes to this project will be documented in this file.

## [2.40 alpha]
### Added
- Ocular Pathology Guide Overhaul: Integrated a complete suite of clinical reference images for all major conditions, including AMD, Diabetic Retinopathy, Retinal Detachment, POAG, AACG, PCAG, Cataracts, and Keratoconus.
- Clinical Lightbox: Added a full-screen, high-resolution visualization tool for all pathology assets, providing an optimized experience for clinical detail recognition on mobile devices.
- Asset Precision: Synchronized the image registry with exact case-sensitive filenames (e.g., POAG.png, Keratoconus.png) to ensure reliable asset loading across all environments.
- Feature Consolidation: Hard-saved and verified the integration of the AI Study Hub (PDF-to-Pro-Notes) and the full Rx Contact Lens Vertex Converter.
